What is our goal?

There is a large amount of people with physical disabilities have a hard time doing grocery shopping as well as postal service queries. This could be due to accessibility and time constraints and people who are physically able tend to take it for granted. There are but a few options for people who cannot go out and get their queries done which is Online shopping and asking friends or family to do so. But online shopping does not always account for accessibility issues and can take a few days for deliveries coming in.
